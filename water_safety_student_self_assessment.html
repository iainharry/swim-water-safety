Reflection Questions

💡 1. What is the most important thing you learned in this unit?
Think about what really surprised you or what you'll actually remember and use.
🔍 2. What is one thing you're still unsure about?
Being honest about this is a sign of strong learning — not weakness.
🌊 3. Describe a real-life situation where you might use what you learned in this unit.
Think about your own life — where do you go near water? What could happen?
🤝 4. Has this unit changed how you think about water safety? Explain.
It's okay to say "not really" — but try to explain why.
⭐ 5. What would you tell a friend who had never done this unit?
The most important thing they'd need to know.
