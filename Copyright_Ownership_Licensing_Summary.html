Important Note — Please Read

This document provides a summary of copyright ownership and licensing information for organisations and resources consulted in the creation of the Swim & Water Safety teaching resource suite. It is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice.

The resources created in this suite draw on factual statistical data, general safety guidelines, and curriculum frameworks. In most cases, use of these materials falls within the educational fair dealing exceptions under the Australian Copyright Act 1968 (Cth), which permits reproduction of copyright material for research, study, criticism, review, or reporting the news, provided the use is "fair and reasonable."

Where resources created in this suite reference, paraphrase, or summarise content from the organisations listed below, this has been done in accordance with applicable licence terms. No substantial portions of any copyrighted material have been reproduced verbatim within the created resources — all content is either: (a) original material created by Em Harrison; (b) factual data cited with appropriate attribution; or (c) general knowledge in the water safety domain not owned by any single organisation.

Copyright licence information was verified in May 2026. Licence terms can change — always check the relevant organisation's current website before reproducing, adapting, or redistributing any materials. If you intend to distribute or publish resources that incorporate content from any of the organisations below, seek written permission where indicated.
