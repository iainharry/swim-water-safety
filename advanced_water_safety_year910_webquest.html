🌐 WebQuest · Year 9–10

Advanced Water Safety
Digital Investigation WebQuest

A guided online investigation across five research stations — exploring Victorian and global drowning data, physiology, social factors, prevention strategies, and international approaches.

Year 9–10 HPE 5 research stations ~50 minutes Devices required VC2HP10P02–VC2HP10P09

🎯 Your Mission

You are a water safety researcher preparing a briefing for the Victorian Government. Over the next 50 minutes you will visit five research stations online, collect evidence, and answer targeted questions at each station. At the end, you will synthesise your findings into a short recommendation.

All fields are optional — complete as much as you can. You will use your findings directly in your Section 6 assessment task.
